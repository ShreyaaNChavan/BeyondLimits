How It Works

At BeyondLimits, we make technology accessible for everyone through AI and assistive tools:

1️⃣ Visual Assistance – AI-powered camera analyzes live visuals and provides answers for the deaf.

2️⃣ Speech-to-Text – Converts spoken words into text, helping those with hearing impairments.

3️⃣ Text-to-Speech – Reads out text for visually impaired users or those with learning difficulties.

4️⃣ ASL Support – Translates American Sign Language into text or speech for seamless communication.

5️⃣ Eye-Tracking Mouse – Enables hands-free device control using just eye movements.
